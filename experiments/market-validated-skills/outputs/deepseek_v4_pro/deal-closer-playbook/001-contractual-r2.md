## Closing Strategy

*Current stage: Evaluation.*  
The goal is to move the deal toward a negotiated close by aligning all stakeholders, removing security roadblocks, and establishing economic justification.

**Strategic moves**:
1. **Security first**: Secure a “pass” from the security team with documented HIPAA audit evidence. This unlocks legal review and removes the known blocker.
2. **Elevate the champion**: Help Maya build an internal business case; connect her with our executive sponsor or customer success manager to present a joint value proposition.
3. **Engage the CFO**: Once security concerns are addressed, schedule a business value review with the CFO, with Maya as advocate. Focus on ROI, risk reduction, and competitive differentiation.
4. **Outflank the incumbent**: Use a “why change” narrative backed by audit‑readiness, user experience, and total cost of ownership. Avoid direct attacks without proof.
5. **Orchestrate the mutual close plan**: Set hard milestones with owners on both sides to hit a 2026‑06‑30 close.
