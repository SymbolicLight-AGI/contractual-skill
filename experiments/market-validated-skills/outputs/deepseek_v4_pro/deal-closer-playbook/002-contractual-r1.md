## Closing Strategy

Given the stage “Demo completed” and missing essential players, the strategy must focus on discovery and building aligned momentum, not a hard close.

**Recommended approach (stage‑appropriate):**
1. **Stakeholder mapping & alignment** – Uncover all committee members, especially economic buyer and technical evaluator; validate Jordan’s role as champion.
2. **Integration deep‑dive** – Address the stated objection head‑on; schedule a technical session to scope integration, provide a proof of concept, and create a shared effort‑value understanding.
3. **Value quantification** – Quantify business impact (cost, time, service) using Acme’s data (to be collected). Build a preliminary business case aligned with the economic buyer’s metrics.
4. **Create a mutual close timeline** – Once budget holder and decision criteria are known, co‑create a closing schedule that respects procurement steps.
5. **Proposal only after above** – Do not present pricing or commit terms until the committee is known and the business case is jointly agreed.

*Important:* The rep should not push for a close date before these foundations are laid; otherwise the deal risks a false close or a no‑decision.
