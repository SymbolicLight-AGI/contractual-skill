## Closing Strategy (Stage: Post-Demo)

Primary objective at this stage is **multi-threading and qualification**, not price negotiation.

1. Convert Jordan into a confirmed champion (validate access to power and willingness to advocate).
2. Get a technical evaluator engaged to defuse the integration objection with facts.
3. Identify and meet the economic buyer; establish budget, timeline, and decision process.
4. Establish a written mutual close plan.
5. Only then move to proposal and commercial terms.
