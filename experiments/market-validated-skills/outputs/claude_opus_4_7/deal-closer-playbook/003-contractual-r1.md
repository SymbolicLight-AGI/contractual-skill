## Closing Strategy

The deal is being driven by prospect-imposed terms rather than a mutual plan. Recommended posture:

1. **Slow the artificial urgency** without losing the deal. Ask what changes if signature slips 3–5 business days.
2. **Decompose the demand.** Treat discount, support scope, and legal terms as three separate workstreams with three different approvers.
3. **Trade, don't give.** Any concession request to leadership should be paired with asks: multi-year term, upfront payment, expansion commitment, reference rights, or scope reduction.
4. **Identify the economic buyer** before escalating any concession internally.
