## 4. Objection Playbook

### KNOWN OBJECTION: "Our security team is worried about HIPAA audit evidence."

**Why they say this:**
The security team needs to defend this purchase decision *to an auditor*. They aren't asking "are you HIPAA-compliant?" (everyone says yes). They're asking: "When an auditor asks for evidence of access controls, audit logs, BAAs, encryption, and breach procedures, can we produce it on demand?" The incumbent is a known quantity in their audit binder. You are not.

**Response framework:**
1. **Acknowledge:** "That's exactly the right question to ask, and frankly it's the question your auditor will ask too."
2. **Reframe:** "The issue isn't whether a vendor is HIPAA-compliant on paper — it's whether you can produce audit evidence in minutes when you need it. Let me show you what that looks like in our platform."
3. **Evidence:** Deliver a **HIPAA Evidence Packet** containing: signed BAA template, SOC 2 Type II report, HITRUST status `[CONFIRM internally]`, audit log sample export, access control documentation, breach notification procedure, sub-processor list, data residency confirmation, encryption-at-rest and in-transit specs.
4. **Confirm:** "If I send this packet over this week, can you put me in front of your security lead for 30 minutes so we can answer their questions directly rather than through a 90-day questionnaire cycle?"

**Example response (scripted):**
> "Maya, that concern is the right one — and it's the same one every healthcare buyer should raise. Here's how I'd suggest we handle it: I'll send you a HIPAA evidence packet by [day] that has everything your security team typically asks for in an audit — BAA, SOC 2, audit log samples, access controls, breach procedures. I'd also like to offer a 30-minute call between your security lead and our compliance team. Most security reviews stall because the questionnaire goes back and forth for weeks. A direct call usually compresses that to one session. Can we set that up?"

**If they push back:**
Offer to complete their HIRPA/security questionnaire on an accelerated timeline (commit to 5 business days) and provide a customer reference at a similarly-sized clinic group whose security team approved you.
