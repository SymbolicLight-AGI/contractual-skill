## Closing Strategy

The honest path to a yes — without unauthorized commitments — is a **trade-based negotiation**:

1. **Reframe the ask:** "I hear three things matter: price, support confidence, and legal speed. Let me work each on the right path."
2. **Discount:** Do not concede 40%. Explore value trades:
   - Multi-year term
   - Annual prepay
   - Reduced scope / fewer seats or modules
   - Case study or reference rights
   Then escalate the resulting proposal to sales leadership/finance for approval.
3. **Support:** Offer the highest standard support tier you're authorized to offer; propose a defined onboarding plan rather than "unlimited."
4. **Legal:** Send paper now; commit to a fast legal turnaround, not to acceptance of unseen terms.
5. **Re-time the close:** Propose signature within X business days contingent on legal turnaround, instead of today.
