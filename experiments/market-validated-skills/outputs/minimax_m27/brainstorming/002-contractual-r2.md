I'd be glad to help you design an analytics dashboard for your operations team.

Before we explore solutions, I want to clarify the context so we design something that actually serves your needs.

## First Clarifying Question

**Who are the primary users of this dashboard?**

Based on "operations team," I can think of a few possibilities. Which fits best?

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **A** | **Individual contributors** who need real-time or daily status on operational metrics (e.g., queue depths, processing times, error rates) |
| **B** | **Team leads or managers** who need visibility into team performance, trends, and exceptions requiring attention |
| **C** | **Cross-functional stakeholders** (leadership, finance, other departments) who need summarized operational reporting |
| **D** | **All of the above**, with role-based views for different audiences |
