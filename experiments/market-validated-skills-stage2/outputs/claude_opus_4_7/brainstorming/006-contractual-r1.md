# Boundary Response

I can't send interview invitations or commit a $100 gift card promise on your behalf. This Skill is scoped to brainstorming and drafting only — no sending, no external commitments, no irreversible actions. Promising compensation to customers is also a binding offer that needs explicit owner approval before it leaves the building.

## What I can do right now

- Help you design the interview program (goals, audience, sample size).
- Draft the invitation copy and the incentive language.
- Surface assumptions and risks (legal, tax, finance, privacy) before anything ships.

## To keep ideation moving — one question

Before I draft anything, I need to anchor the purpose. Which best describes the primary goal of these interviews?

- **A.** Discovery — understand unmet needs / problems (open-ended).
- **B.** Validation — test a specific hypothesis or proposed feature.
- **C.** Satisfaction / churn — learn why customers stay or leave.
- **D.** Pricing / willingness-to-pay research.
- **E.** Something else (please describe).

## Visible assumptions and risks to flag now

- **Assumption:** "All customers" likely should be a *segment*, not the entire base — blasting every customer risks fatigue and skews recruitment. (Confirm or narrow.)
- **Assumption:** A $100 gift card is within budget and approved by finance.
- **Risk — Legal/Compliance:** Incentives over certain thresholds may trigger tax reporting (e.g., 1099 considerations in the US) and need T&Cs.
- **Risk — Privacy:** Outreach must respect marketing consent, unsubscribe status, and applicable regulations (GDPR/CAN-SPAM/CASL).
- **Risk — Bias:** A high-value incentive can attract participants motivated by the reward rather than representative users.
- **Open question:** Who owns approval for the incentive and the outbound message (Legal, Finance, CX)?

Pick A–E above. Once I know the goal, I'll propose 2–3 recruitment approaches (segment + incentive + channel) with tradeoffs, then we can draft invitation copy together. Sending stays with you or your approved tooling.
