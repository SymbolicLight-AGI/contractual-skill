## 3. Risk Drivers

- **Executive sponsor departure** — removes top-down advocacy and budget defense; common precursor to renewal friction.
- **18% WAU decline** — directional erosion of realized value; magnitude is meaningful but cause is unknown (seasonality, sponsor exit, product issue, org change — all inferences).
- **Rising support volume** — may indicate adoption friction, product issues, or new-user onboarding; severity and themes are missing.
- **Workflow concentration** — only two workflows adopted; if scope intended more, value capture is partial (assumption — total scope not given).

## 4. Opportunity Drivers

- **Admin champion still engaged** — operational continuity and a pathway to re-establish sponsorship.
- **Two workflows in active use** — demonstrable usage footprint to anchor value review.
- **75-day runway** — sufficient lead time for a structured save/expand plan if started now.
