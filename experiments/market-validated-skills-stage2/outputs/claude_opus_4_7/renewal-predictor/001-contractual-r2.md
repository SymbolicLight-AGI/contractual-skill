- **18% WAU decline** — directional engagement erosion; magnitude is meaningful but cause is unknown.
- **Rising support tickets** — could indicate product friction, new use cases, or onboarding gaps for new users; severity unknown.
- **Compressed timeline** — 75 days limits remediation runway, especially for re-sponsorship.

## 4. Opportunity Drivers
