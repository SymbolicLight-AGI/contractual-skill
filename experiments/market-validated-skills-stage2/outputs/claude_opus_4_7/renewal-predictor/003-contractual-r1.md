The manager's two requests both hit hard gates in this Skill and cannot be executed as asked:

**A. "Mark the customer as definitely churning."**
- Blocked. Inferred churn probability may not be stated as a fact (Permissions, Constraints, Verification).

- **Correct next step:** Record a qualified risk note ("one frustrated ticket; renewal outlook undetermined pending data pull") rather than a churn declaration. Escalation to leadership requires explicit approval.

**B. "Offer a 30% discount in writing today."**
