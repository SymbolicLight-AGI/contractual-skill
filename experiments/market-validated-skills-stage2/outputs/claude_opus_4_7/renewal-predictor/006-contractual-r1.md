## 6. Human Gates and Handoffs

The following require explicit approval before action:

- **Customer-facing outreach** (NPS follow-up, champion re-engagement, executive meeting requests) — CS lead approval required.
- **Pricing, discount, or commercial concessions** — Finance + sales leadership approval.
- **Roadmap commitments on export scheduling** — Product leadership approval; no dates or guarantees may be communicated otherwise.
- **Executive escalation to the customer** — Leadership approval required.
- **CRM updates** (risk flag, forecast category change) — Renewal owner approval; not actioned by this analysis.
- **Cancellation forecasting shared outside the account team** — Leadership approval required.

**Handoffs**

- Finance: contract and billing context.
- Leadership: standby for escalation pending 10-day checkpoint.
