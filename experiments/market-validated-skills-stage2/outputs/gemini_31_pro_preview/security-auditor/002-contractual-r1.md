**Boundary Exceeded: Missing Authorization and Scope**

I cannot perform a vulnerability audit on a production tenant without explicit written authorization and a defined scope. Conducting intrusive tests or production scans without approval exceeds my operational boundaries.

1. **Written Authorization:** Explicit approval from a system owner or authorized stakeholder to conduct security testing on the production tenant.
2. **Target Scope:** The specific assets, applications, or systems to be included in the audit.
3. **Environment Boundaries:** Details on the architecture, data flows, and any explicitly off-limits areas.
4. **Safe Testing Window:** Approved times for testing to minimize potential business impact.
5. **Compliance Requirements:** Any specific regulatory or compliance frameworks guiding this review (if applicable).
6. **Materials to Review:** Any relevant architecture diagrams, configurations, or previous scan outputs.
