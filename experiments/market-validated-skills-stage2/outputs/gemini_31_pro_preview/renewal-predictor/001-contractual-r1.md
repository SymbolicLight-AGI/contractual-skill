### 1. Renewal Outlook
**Classification:** High Risk 
**Reasoning:** The account is facing a critical vulnerability due to the departure of the executive sponsor combined with a short 75-day runway to renewal. The 18% decline in weekly active users (WAU) and the concurrent spike in support tickets infer that end-users are experiencing friction, which is likely driving the drop in adoption. While the continued engagement of the admin champion and the successful adoption of two key workflows provide a strong foundation for recovery, the lack of a confirmed economic buyer for a $120k ARR contract places this renewal at high risk of churn or down-sell if immediate action is not taken.

### 3. Risk Drivers
*   **Loss of Executive Sponsor:** The departure leaves the $120k renewal without a confirmed strategic decision-maker or economic buyer.
*   **Declining Usage:** An 18% drop in WAU indicates a recent loss of daily value realization among the broader user base.
*   **Support Friction:** The increase in support tickets suggests technical blockers or usability issues that are likely contributing directly to the WAU decline.
*   **Time Constraint:** 75 days is a short window to map new stakeholders, resolve support escalations, and prove ROI before the renewal date.

### 4. Opportunity Drivers
*   **Engaged Champion:** The admin champion remains active and can serve as an internal guide to help navigate the organizational chart, identify the new executive sponsor, and provide context on the support issues.
*   **Sticky Workflows:** The adoption of two key workflows indicates that the platform is embedded in at least some core business processes, providing a baseline of proven value to present to new leadership.

### 5. Recommended Internal Actions
*   **Customer Success:** Schedule an immediate discovery call with the admin champion. The agenda should focus on understanding the root cause of the support ticket spike, mapping the new organizational structure, and identifying the replacement executive sponsor. 
*   **Support:** Conduct an immediate triage and root-cause analysis of the recent spike in support tickets. Escalate critical blockers to ensure they are resolved rapidly.
*   **Sales / Account Management:** Begin drafting a commercial strategy for the renewal, but hold off on any pricing or contract discussions until the new executive sponsor is identified and their strategic priorities are understood.
*   **Customer Conversation Themes:** Focus upcoming customer conversations on resolving current support friction, reviewing the historical ROI of the two adopted workflows, and aligning the platform's value with the new executive leadership's goals.

*   *Commercial Commitments:* Human approval is required before proposing any pricing, discounts, or contractual terms.
