## 5. Recommended internal actions and handoffs

### Customer Success handoff

**Recommended owner:** CS / account owner  
**Objective:** Rebuild stakeholder alignment and renewal confidence.

Actions:
- Confirm who replaced or inherited responsibilities from the former admin champion.
- Build or refresh the stakeholder map: admin, business owner, executive sponsor, technical owner, procurement contact.
- Schedule an internal account review before any customer-facing outreach.
- Prepare a customer conversation focused on:
  - Understanding the impact of unresolved integrations.
  - Clarifying reasons for NPS decline.
  - Determining whether export scheduling is a renewal blocker.
  - Identifying current business outcomes and adoption gaps.
- Validate renewal intent directly with appropriate customer stakeholders, but do not represent inferred churn risk as fact.

### Support handoff

**Recommended owner:** Support lead / technical support manager  
**Objective:** Resolve or create a credible path for unresolved integration tickets.

Actions:
- Audit all open integration tickets tied to the account.
- Identify ticket age, severity, root cause, dependencies, and customer impact.
- Assign a technical owner for each unresolved issue.
- Provide CS with a remediation timeline and any blockers.
- Escalate internally if unresolved tickets are business-critical or renewal-blocking.

### Product handoff

**Recommended owner:** Product manager for export/reporting workflows  
**Objective:** Assess the export scheduling gap and provide a realistic product position.

Actions:
- Confirm whether export scheduling is on the roadmap, already planned, not planned, or available through workaround.
- Provide CS with approved internal guidance on:
  - Current capability.
  - Known limitations.
  - Possible workaround, if one exists and is approved.
  - What cannot be promised.
- Determine whether the gap is tied to broader reporting, automation, or compliance requirements.

### Finance handoff

**Recommended owner:** Finance / billing operations  
**Objective:** Confirm there are no financial or procurement blockers.

Actions:
- Verify billing remains current.
- Check for invoice disputes, pending credits, payment timing issues, or procurement flags.
- Confirm renewal process requirements if known internally.
- Do not offer discounts, credits, or modified payment terms without approval.

### Leadership handoff

**Recommended owner:** CS leadership / account leadership  
**Objective:** Decide escalation level and support cross-functional rescue plan.

Actions:
- Review account risk given the 45-day renewal window.
- Decide whether executive involvement is warranted.
- Approve any leadership-to-leadership engagement before outreach.
- Ensure Support and Product commitments are realistic and internally approved.
- Determine whether the account requires a formal renewal rescue standup until renewal close.
