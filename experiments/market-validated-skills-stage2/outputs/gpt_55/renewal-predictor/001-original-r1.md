## Renewal Prediction

This account is **At Risk**. The account has enough product value to save because the admin champion is still engaged and two key workflows are adopted. However, the combination of **usage decline**, **increasing support burden**, and **executive sponsor loss** creates material renewal risk, especially with renewal only **75 days away**.

## Active Churn Signals

- **Executive sponsor departure:** The executive sponsor has left, creating stakeholder continuity risk and potential loss of budget-level advocacy.
- **Usage decline:** Weekly active users are down **18%**, approaching the 20% threshold that would indicate a stronger usage-trend risk.
- **Support volume increase:** Support tickets are up, suggesting either product friction, implementation issues, or user frustration.
- **Renewal proximity:** Renewal is within 90 days, which raises urgency even though the health score is not yet in likely-churn territory.

## Positive / Saveable Signals

- **Admin champion remains engaged:** There is still an active internal advocate who can help rebuild executive alignment.
- **Two key workflows adopted:** The product is not unused; there is existing operational dependency that can be reinforced in the renewal conversation.

## Recommended Save Play

**Priority:** High  
**Action:** Stakeholder Re-engagement Campaign + Executive Sponsor Alignment  
**Owner:** CSM with CS Leader support  
**Deadline:** Within 10 business days  
**Expected Outcome:** Re-establish executive sponsorship, confirm renewal criteria, and stabilize usage before renewal discussions intensify.

### Specific Next Steps

1. **Schedule a renewal alignment meeting with the engaged admin champion within 5 business days.**
   - Goal: Identify who replaced the executive sponsor, who owns budget, and what concerns are driving support volume.

2. **Ask the champion for an introduction to the new executive or decision-maker.**
   - Position the meeting as a partnership and value review, not a renewal-save attempt.

3. **Prepare a value summary before the executive meeting.**
   - Include the two adopted workflows, business outcomes tied to those workflows, current usage decline, and a proposed 60-day recovery plan.

4. **Audit recent support tickets this week.**
   - Determine whether ticket growth is due to bugs, enablement gaps, workflow confusion, or unmet product expectations.

5. **Launch a 30-day usage recovery plan.**
   - Target: reverse WAU decline from -18% to flat or positive before renewal.
   - Focus: reinforce the two adopted workflows and identify one additional high-value workflow to activate.

## Bottom Line

**Renewal is at risk but recoverable.** The highest-leverage move is to use the engaged admin champion to quickly rebuild executive sponsorship, diagnose the support-ticket increase, and create a short-term usage recovery plan before the renewal window tightens.
