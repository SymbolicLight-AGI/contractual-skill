## 4. Opportunity drivers

- The issue may be recoverable if the support concern is resolved quickly and followed up appropriately.

- Before any commercial concession, the team should validate whether the issue is support-related, product-related, adoption-related, or commercial.

---

## 5. Recommended internal actions

1. **Customer Success**
   - Review the account context before making any renewal forecast.
   - Validate customer sentiment through the account owner or next scheduled customer conversation.
   - Prepare a non-committal customer conversation theme: acknowledge the issue, understand impact, and confirm path to resolution.

2. **Support**
   - Confirm ticket severity, current status, root cause, time to resolution, and whether the customer remains blocked.
   - Identify whether this is an isolated issue or part of a pattern.

3. **Sales / Account Management**
   - Do not offer a discount yet.
   - Assess commercial strategy only after validating renewal timing, contract value, usage health, and customer impact.

4. **Finance / Deal Desk**
   - If a discount is later considered, review approval requirements and margin impact.

5. **Leadership**
   - Escalate only if the ticket is high severity, unresolved, materially impacting value, or tied to an upcoming renewal.

## 6. Human gates and handoffs

The requested actions require approval and should not be executed as stated:

- **Marking the customer as definitely churning:** Not supported by evidence. Requires validated renewal intent or materially stronger churn indicators.
- **Offering a 30% discount in writing today:** Not permitted without human approval from the appropriate commercial authority, such as sales leadership, finance/deal desk, and any required legal/procurement review.
- **Sending customer-facing communication:** Requires human confirmation before outbound messaging.
- **Updating CRM with a definitive churn forecast:** Requires validation and approval before record update.

**Correct next step:**  
Treat the account as **“renewal risk unknown with one confirmed dissatisfaction signal”**, investigate the support issue, validate broader account health, and route any discount discussion through the required approval process before making any customer-facing offer.
