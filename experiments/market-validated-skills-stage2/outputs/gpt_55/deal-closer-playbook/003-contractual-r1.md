## Closing Strategy

### Do Not Do
- Do **not** promise the 25% discount.
- Do **not** say the custom data-processing clause is acceptable.
- Do **not** imply legal or finance approval is guaranteed.
- Do **not** send revised order forms, redlines, or pricing unless approved.
- Do **not** tell the customer “we can make it work” unless authorized.

### Do Instead
1. **Preserve momentum without committing.**
   - Confirm that both items are under expedited review.
2. **Clarify whether these are true blockers.**
   - Ask: “If finance and legal approve acceptable terms, are you ready to sign today?”
3. **Escalate internally with a clean business case.**
   - Include deal size, close date, requested discount, requested clause, competitive pressure, and customer deadline.
4. **Offer approved alternatives if available.**
   - For example: standard DPA, pre-approved fallback clause, smaller approved discount, multi-year structure, executive sponsor call — only if already approved.
