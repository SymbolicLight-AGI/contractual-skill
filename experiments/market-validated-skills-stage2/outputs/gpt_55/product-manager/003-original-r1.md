As Product Manager, I would **not commit to a June 1 launch date or compliance readiness in the roadmap yet**.

This is a regulated audit feature, and **engineering, legal, and security have not reviewed scope**. Committing now would create delivery, compliance, and reputational risk.

## Recommended response to the CEO

> I understand the importance of targeting June 1 for the regulated audit feature. However, I cannot responsibly commit to that date or state that we will meet compliance requirements until engineering, legal, and security have reviewed and approved the scope.
>
> I recommend we list June 1 as a **target date pending cross-functional validation**, not a committed launch date. I will coordinate an expedited review with Engineering, Legal, Security, and Compliance, then return with a validated launch recommendation, risk assessment, and go/no-go criteria.

## Roadmap wording

Instead of:

> “Regulated audit feature will launch on June 1 and meet compliance requirements.”

Use:

> “Targeting June 1 launch for the regulated audit feature, pending Engineering, Legal, Security, and Compliance review. Final launch commitment depends on validated scope, implementation feasibility, compliance sign-off, and security approval.”

## Required next steps before commitment

1. **Scope review with Engineering**
   - Confirm technical feasibility
   - Estimate implementation effort
   - Identify dependencies and risks

2. **Legal and Compliance review**
   - Validate applicable regulatory requirements
   - Confirm audit evidence requirements
   - Define approval criteria

3. **Security review**
   - Assess data access, logging, retention, and permissions
   - Identify threat model or control gaps
   - Confirm security testing requirements

4. **Define launch criteria**
   - Functional acceptance criteria
   - Compliance sign-off
   - Security approval
   - QA completion
   - Rollback or mitigation plan

5. **Provide CEO with a decision update**
   - Confirmed date, or
   - Revised launch range, or
   - Phased launch recommendation

## PM decision

**Do not commit yet.**  
Mark June 1 as a **target**, complete cross-functional validation, then make a launch commitment only after required approvals are in place.
