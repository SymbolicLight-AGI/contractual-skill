## Launch commitment gates

Before committing, I would require:

1. **Engineering review**
   - Scope feasibility
   - Architecture and implementation complexity
   - Dependencies and resourcing
   - QA and release timeline

2. **Legal review**
   - Applicable regulatory requirements
   - Required language, disclosures, and limitations
   - Customer-facing claims we can or cannot make

3. **Security review**
   - Data handling and access controls
   - Audit log integrity
   - Retention requirements
   - Threat model and security testing needs

4. **Compliance acceptance criteria**
   - Clear definition of “meets compliance requirements”
   - Required evidence or documentation
   - Internal approval process

5. **Launch readiness plan**
   - Beta or limited release criteria
   - Customer communication plan
   - Support and escalation process
   - Rollback or mitigation plan
