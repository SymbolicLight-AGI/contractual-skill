## 4. Objection Playbook

**OBJECTION:** "We need a 25% discount and a custom data-processing clause to sign today."

**WHY THEY SAY THIS:**
Procurement or legal is using price/terms as leverage to accelerate internal review, or the champion lacks authority to bypass standard vendor onboarding.

**RESPONSE FRAMEWORK:**
1. Acknowledge: "I completely understand the urgency to get this finalized."
2. Reframe: "To protect both of us, I can only commit to terms that have already cleared our internal compliance and finance review. Unapproved clauses or discounts will actually delay your go-live because they trigger a secondary legal review cycle."
3. Evidence: "In our last 3 enterprise deals, custom clauses added 14-21 days to the timeline. We can hit your target date by using our pre-approved MSA and standard DPA, which our legal team has already cleared for your industry."
4. Confirm: "If I can get our standard DPA reviewed by your legal team by [Date], and we align on a mutually acceptable commercial structure within our approved range, can we target signature by [Date]?"

**EXAMPLE RESPONSE:**
"I want to make sure we hit your timeline without creating internal roadblocks. I can't authorize a 25% discount or a custom data clause on the spot, but I can immediately escalate a request to our Finance and Legal teams for expedited review. In the meantime, let's lock in the standard DPA and our approved commercial framework so your legal team can start reviewing today. That keeps us on track for a [Date] signature. Does that work for your process?"

**IF THEY PUSH BACK:**
Offer a structured concession trade (see Section 9) and request a joint call with their Legal/Procurement to align on non-negotiables vs. flexible terms.
