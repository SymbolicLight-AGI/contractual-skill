- **Objection:** "We need a 25% discount to sign today."
  - **Response:** Acknowledge budget constraints. Explain that all pricing exceptions require finance review to maintain compliance and service quality. Offer standard alternatives (e.g., extended payment terms, phased rollout, or standard volume tiers) while routing the request internally.
- **Objection:** "We require a custom data-processing clause immediately."
