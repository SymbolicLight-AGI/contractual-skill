## Objection Playbook
- **Objection:** "We need a 25% discount to sign today."
  - **Response:** Acknowledge budget constraints. Explain that pricing governance requires Finance approval for discounts exceeding standard thresholds. Offer alternative value levers (e.g., extended payment terms, phased rollout, or included onboarding credits) while routing the exception request.
  - **Action:** Submit formal discount exception to Finance with business justification.
- **Objection:** "We require a custom data-processing clause."
  - **Response:** Acknowledge compliance requirements. State that all custom clauses must undergo Legal/Security review to align with company standards and regulatory obligations. Provide standard DPA/security addendum as interim step.
  - **Action:** Route clause to Legal intake with clear timeline pressure.
- **Anticipated:** "If you can't do this, we'll walk."
  - **Response:** Reiterate commitment to partnership and compliance. Propose a mutual close plan with transparent internal approval timelines. Escalate to Executive Sponsor if strategic account value justifies expedited exception process.
