- **Unverified billing/contract status:** Unknown payment history or contract terms may hide administrative or financial blockers.
- **Lack of support context:** Unresolved technical or operational issues could be impacting perceived value without visibility.

4. Opportunity drivers:
- **Active baseline usage:** Confirms the product is deployed and in use, providing a foundation for value realization conversations.
- **Upcoming renewal window:** Creates a natural timeline to initiate health checks, validate stakeholder alignment, and secure retention or expansion.

5. Recommended internal actions:
- **Data collection:** Pull internal CRM, usage analytics, and billing records to populate missing signals.
- **Stakeholder mapping:** Identify primary users, economic buyers, and executive sponsors from account records.
- **Engagement planning:** Draft a discovery agenda focused on usage trends, satisfaction, and upcoming business goals.
- **Internal alignment:** Schedule a brief sync between CS, Sales, and Finance to assign data validation owners before customer outreach.

6. Human gates and handoffs:
- **Human gates:** Require human confirmation before any customer-facing outreach, pricing/discount discussions, renewal forecasting shared externally, or CRM record updates. Do not treat current assessment as a final churn/renewal probability.
- **Handoffs:** 
  - Customer Success: Lead data gathering and draft customer engagement plan.
