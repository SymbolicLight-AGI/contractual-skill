## Assessment Summary

**What is known:** The account has 75 days to renewal, $120k ARR at stake, declining usage and rising support volume, and a critical sponsor gap. The admin champion retention is the primary stabilizing factor.

**What is unknown:** Customer sentiment, billing status, feature satisfaction, and detailed usage breakdown. Filling these gaps will sharpen the risk score.

**What needs human approval:** Any outreach to new executives, pricing adjustments, contract modifications, or external churn forecasts.

**Bottom line:** This account requires immediate engagement prioritization. The admin champion is the entry point; re-establishing executive trust is the precondition for renewal confidence. The usage decline warrants root-cause analysis before the next customer conversation.
