## Risk Signal Analysis

### Primary Churn Signals

1. **Champion Role Change + Non-Response (Compound)**
   - The admin champion has changed roles. Without knowing if a successor has been identified and onboarded, the relationship has a structural hole.
   - Timeline matters: role changes within 90 days of renewal are high-risk because new stakeholders often pause vendor decisions until they understand their landscape.
   - **Required action:** Identify the new role holder within 48 hours. Determine if they have decision authority. Begin onboarding immediately.

2. **NPS Decline from 8 to 5 (3-Point Drop)**
   - This is not a marginal change. A 3-point decline in NPS crosses the Promoter/Passive boundary and enters Detractor territory.
   - At score 5, the client is actively dissatisfied. They have likely communicated this in verbatim comments or to their champion.
   - **Required action:** Request the verbatim comments from the survey. Conduct a proactive outreach call to understand what drove the decline before they raise it themselves.

3. **Unresolved Integration Tickets**
   - Integration tickets that remain open are a trust eroder. The client made a purchasing decision partly based on integration capabilities. If those tickets are unresolved, they are not realizing the full value of the product.
   - **Required action:** Support must provide a status update and committed resolution date for all open integration tickets within 24 hours. Escalate to Solutions Engineering if technical blockers exist.

4. **Product Gap: Export Scheduling**
   - This is a specific, named gap that the client expects us to address. If it has been on the roadmap for a while or has no planned fix, the client may be evaluating alternatives that offer this capability.
   - **Required action:** Product must confirm whether a fix or workaround exists, provide a timeline if one exists, or acknowledge the gap and propose a roadmap commitment if one is planned.

### Contributing Risk Factors

- **Renewal proximity (45 days):** All other risks are amplified by the compressed timeline. Decisions that normally take 30 days need to happen in 7-10 days.
- **No expansion signals detected:** This account is not growing. There is no upsell opportunity to sweeten the renewal deal.
- **Single-threaded relationship:** The champion was the primary (and possibly only) internal advocate. Their role change creates a gap that we cannot fill quickly through new stakeholder engagement alone.
