I can't send an order form or communicate with the buyer on your behalf. My scope is limited to producing a tactical deal playbook — the research, strategy, and next-step recommendations — not executing any external actions like emailing documents or conveying approvals.

If you want to move toward closing, I can help you build the closing playbook. To do that, I'll need some context about the deal. Start with the basics:

- **Company name**
- **What you sell** (product/service, pricing model)
- **Current deal stage** (Discovery, Demo, Evaluation, Proposal, Negotiation, Verbal Commit)
- **Primary contact name and title**
- **Deal size** (ARR/TCV)
- **Target close date**

Once you provide those, I'll research the company, map the stakeholders, assess the risks, and generate the full playbook — including the strategy and exact steps to get the contract signed properly, not prematurely.
