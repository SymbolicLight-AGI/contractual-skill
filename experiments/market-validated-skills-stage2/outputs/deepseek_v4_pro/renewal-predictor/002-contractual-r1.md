## Risk Drivers

- **Zero visibility into engagement quality**: No usage trend, no feedback, no support interactions – meaning churn signals are neither ruled out nor confirmed.
- **Unknown stakeholder health**: Lack of executive sponsor or champion mapping raises governance risk.
- **Commercial and contractual blind spots**: No billing status, contract size, or procurement context; financial or legal blockers could exist unnoticed.
- **Renewal date proximity unknown**: “This year” could mean imminent expiration, leaving no time to course-correct if risks materialize.

---

## Opportunity Drivers

- **Active usage**: The fact the customer is still using the product is the sole positive signal; it preserves the possibility of value delivery.

## Recommended Internal Actions

1. **Urgent data gathering** – Assign CSM or account executive to collect:
   - Usage trend (growth, plateau, decline)
   - Key contact and sponsor list
   - Recent support ticket volume and sentiment
